# Chandigarh University - MCA (AI & ML) Syllabus and Information

## Program Overview
The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Chandigarh University is a two-year postgraduate program designed to build strong foundations in modern computing. 
The curriculum focuses heavily on practical applications of deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

## Fee Structure
The tuition fee for the MCA program is ₹80,000 per semester. Additional fees include an examination fee of ₹2,000 per semester and a one-time security deposit of ₹5,000 (refundable).
Hostel fees range from ₹1,05,000 to ₹1,40,000 per year depending on the AC/Non-AC room category chosen.

## Semester 1
Core subjects in the first semester include:
- Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms
- Foundations of Artificial Intelligence
- Python Programming for Data Science
- Database Management Systems

## Semester 2
The second semester introduces more specialized topics:
- Machine Learning Techniques
- Big Data Analytics
- Cloud Computing Architecture
- Software Engineering Principles

Students must also complete a mini-project applying machine learning to a real-world dataset.

## Admission Process
Admission is based on the CUCET (Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test) score. Scholarships up to 100% are available for top scorers in CUCET. Minimum eligibility is a Bachelor's degree with 50% aggregate marks, preferably with Mathematics at the 10+2 level.
